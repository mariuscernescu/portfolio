About

Hey, thank you for visiting my website. I’m Marius, a Front end developer currently based in Brasov, Romania, and here is a short insight into my journey.

Technology and computers always fascinated me. I was that kid who would disassemble my first computer just to find out how all the pieces work together. Later, I was that friend you call when you need to troubleshoot some error on your device. I would spend most of my free time testing out a new Linux system, learning about information security and keeping up to date with all the latest technology. But somehow, I never got into learning how to code.

Until five months ago. After many years of living abroad, I found myself back in Romania after my inernational travel plans got cancelled because of the pandemic. I took this as a chance to build a new career path and enrolled in a begginer web development course. A few days later I was hooked and commited to #100DaysOfCode challenge on Twitter.
